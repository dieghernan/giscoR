======================
   GISCO Ports 2009
======================


Data
=====

The GISCO Ports 2009 data is disseminated in ESRI Geodatabase format (Access database) and shapefile.


Metadata
=========

The metadata follows the ISO 19139 recommendations. It is provided in XML format.

To visualise the PORT metadata just open the file "PORT-MetadataCatalog.htm" in your web browser.

Due to the restrictions in the length of the attribute names in shapefiles (max. 10 characters), some attribute names in the metadata might slightly differ from the actual attribute names in the shapefiles. 


Data source and copyright acknowledgement.
===========================================

GISCO Ports is a geographical dataset developed by the European Commission. When the GISCO Ports geographical dataset is used in any printed or electronic publication, the source data set must be acknowledged in the legend of the map and in the introductory page of the publication.

The data source notice on the legend of the map and the introductory part of the publication is as follows:

	Data source: Eurostat - GISCO (European Commission)



Copyright clauses for geographical data derived from data developed at the European Environment Agency (EEA).
=============================================================================================================

The GISCO Ports geographical dataset contains geometry derived from Corine Land Cover 2000 (CLC2000) developed by the EEA together with its member countries. 

Where polygon geometry from the GISCO Ports geographical dataset is used in any printed or electronic publication, the source data set (CLC2000) must be acknowledged in the legend of the map and in the introductory page of the publication, next to the European Commission data source acknowledgement.

The copyright notice on the legend of the map and the introductory part of the publication is as follows:

© European Environment Agency for CORINE Land Cover 2000

For publications in languages other than English, the translation of the appropriate copyright notices in the language of the publication shall be used.
